# ThreatSense - Product Brief

## Executive Summary

ThreatSense is an automated security platform that democratizes enterprise-grade cybersecurity for small and medium-sized businesses. By combining SOCaaS (Security Operations Center as a Service), PTaaS (Penetration Testing as a Service), and continuous vulnerability scanning into a single, intuitive platform, ThreatSense delivers comprehensive security at a fraction of traditional costs.

---

## The Problem

### Market Gap
- **33 million small businesses** in the US need cybersecurity protection
- **60% of small businesses** close within 6 months of a cyber attack
- Traditional security services cost **$50,000-200,000/year**
- Small businesses simply **cannot afford** enterprise security solutions

### Current Pain Points
1. **Too Expensive**: Enterprise SOC services start at $50K/year
2. **Too Complex**: Require dedicated security teams to operate
3. **Too Slow**: Manual penetration tests take weeks to schedule
4. **Limited Access**: Small businesses are completely underserved

#### 3. **Continuous Vulnerability Scanning**
- Automated daily scans

- **Value**: Replaces $10-20K/year vulnerability management
